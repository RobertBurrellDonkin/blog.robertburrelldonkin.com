My break into software development was in HR solutions. Working with for three years with real domain experts
dedicated to improve the state of the art gave me life-long interest.

---

Fairness is all about value judgements. It’s a hard time to be in HR, I think. Who to include and who to exclude. In times like this, perhaps even who lives and who dies…
Being fair and inclusive to candidates without expertise in engineering challenging solutions may mean excluding specialists with proven track records of successful delivery.

A recent interview required that I pick a project simple enough to be explained fully in 5 minutes.

But every project I’ve been involved with has been complex and challenging.
